I'm Jawad Akram, a creative graphic designer and digital artist specializing in vector illustration, seamless patterns, and commercial stock art. My work is available to designers and creatives worldwide through leading stock platforms.

With a deep passion for bold visual storytelling, I create assets that range from vibrant seamless patterns and hand-drawn illustrations to tribal tattoo designs, silhouette art, and holiday graphics — all crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

My portfolio spans Adobe Stock, Shutterstock, and Behance, where I continuously push the boundaries of commercial vector art, making high-quality design accessible to creators globally.
